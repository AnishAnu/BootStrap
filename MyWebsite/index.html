KappaTV is malayalam music channel from the Mathrubhumi group, the publisher of the widely circulated Mathrubhumi daily. The channel was named after the local dish made from Tapioca, which is called kappa in Malayalam language. The channel focus on youth oriented programs and with a great emphasis on music.
